Roguelite defense game

Explore -> Defend -> Upgrade -> Extract -> Repeat....

Design pillars
    Impactful upgrades - every upgrade influences gameplay significantly, not just stat boosting
    Diverse strategy - Synergy from upgrades allow for certain strategies to emerge

Core systems
    Procedural worlds
        Different enemies  
        Different resources
        New tower unlocks
            Fire world - fire tower
            Ice world - aoe
            Poison world - Damage over time
    Unlocks and upgrades
        New towers
        Upgrade existing towers
        Upgrade items
    Building defenses
    Player combat


Flow
1) Spawn in world
2) Place base 
3) Wave of enemies 
4) Unlock towers, upgrades
5) Choose when to extract , repeat

Upgrades
    Towers
        Simple stat upgrades
        Change the actual mechanics of tower
        Tower  stats:
            Attack damage
            Attack speed
            Pierce
            Knockback
            etc..
    Player
        Simple stat upgrades
        Player stats:
            Health
            Speed
            Build range
            Abilities?
    Items
        Simple stat upgrades
        Attack damage
        Attack speed
        Pierce
        Knockback
        etc..

Building items / resources
    Coins
    Enemy drops (unique to different worlds)
    Map resource 

Defenses
    Ranged
        High fire rate, low damage
        High damage, slow fire rate
        AOE
    Melee 
    Spawner
    Stun
    Crowd control
        Stun
        Poison / fire

Enemies
    Basic enemy
    Fast moving low hp
    Slow moving, high hp
    Healers



Strategies
    Damage over time
    AOE
    Crowd control / execute
    etc...
